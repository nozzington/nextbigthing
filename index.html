What is The Next Big Thing?

This is my ongoing project log ranging from professional to personal endeavours. Essentially, this is for me to both track and show everyone my dreams and how I am achieving them so others can do the same.

I am a 23 year old masters student in advanced aeronautical engineering that wants to start their own company in the space sector and change industry attitudes and be the innovator that builds humanity's foundations of exploration with roots in sustainability.

I want the lessons to be evident and my personality to come through, really this is for me to keep myself motivated and to remember what I want from life: to keep getting better and focus on THE NEXT BIG THING.
